How It Works

  1. Create Your Album: Start by giving your memory album a name — maybe it's a birthday, a wedding, or just a fun hangout with friends. With one simple click, your private digital album is ready to capture every special moment.
  2. Invite Your People: Once your album is ready, you’ll get a unique private code. Share it with anyone you want — no sign-up needed — anyone with the code can join and start uploading their memories!
  3. Upload from Any Device: Whether you're using a phone, tablet, or computer, uploading photos is quick and easy. Everyone can add their best moments to the album — anytime, anywhere.
  4. Relive the Moments: Watch your album grow as each photo is added. Flip through your shared collection, laugh at the memories, and download your favorite shots. It’s like building a digital scrapbook — together.
